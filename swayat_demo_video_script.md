# Swayat AI Demo Video Script

## Video 1: Main Product Demo (60 seconds)
**Best for:** Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, X, Product Hunt
**Format:** Screen recording of WhatsApp on phone (vertical, 9:16)

### Setup
- Use your real WhatsApp with the Swayat bot
- Clean up the chat first (or start a fresh session with /reset)
- Use a good screen recorder (iPhone: built-in, Android: AZ Screen Recorder)
- Turn on Do Not Disturb so no notifications pop in

### Script + Actions

**[0-5 sec] — Hook**
Text overlay on screen: "I run my entire business from WhatsApp."
(Show the WhatsApp chat open with Swayat)

**[5-15 sec] — Invoicing**
Type: "Create invoice for Sarah Chen, $2,500 for brand identity package, due in 30 days"
Wait for AI response showing the formatted invoice.
Text overlay: "Invoices in 10 seconds."

**[15-25 sec] — Booking**
Type: "Book a call with Mike tomorrow at 2pm"
Wait for AI to confirm + show calendar sync.
Text overlay: "Appointments auto-synced to Google Calendar."

**[25-35 sec] — Lead Management**
Type: "What hot leads came in this week?"
Wait for AI response showing scored leads.
Text overlay: "AI scores every lead automatically."

**[35-45 sec] — Marketing**
Type: "Draft an Instagram post about our spring sale — 20% off all services"
Wait for AI response with the drafted post.
Text overlay: "Marketing content in seconds."

**[45-55 sec] — CTA**
Text overlay on green background:
"7 business tools. One WhatsApp chat. Free."
"swayat.com"

**[55-60 sec] — End card**
Swayat logo + "Start free at swayat.com"

## Recording Tips

1. **Phone orientation:** Record vertical (9:16) for Reels/Shorts/TikTok
2. **Clean chat:** Start with /reset so the demo looks clean
3. **Type slowly enough** that viewers can read what you're typing
4. **Wait for the AI response** — the magic is seeing it work in real time
5. **Good lighting** if doing face cam portions
6. **Background music:** Use trending audio on Instagram/TikTok for more reach
7. **Captions:** Add text captions — 80% of people watch with sound off
8. **First 3 seconds matter:** The hook needs to stop the scroll
