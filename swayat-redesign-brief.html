prompt for claude · paste verbatim

"Replace the flat header with a floating glass pill."

Context: the current header is <header class="fixed top-0 w-full border-b bg-white/80 backdrop-blur-lg"> — a full-width bar. Target: a floating rounded-full pill that sits 16–24px from the top, max-width ~1200px, centered. Use these tokens: background: rgba(255,255,255,0.72) with backdrop-blur(20px) saturate(140%) border: 1px solid rgba(15,23,42,0.06) shadow: 0 1px 0 rgba(255,255,255,0.6) inset, 0 20px 40px -20px rgba(15,23,42,0.20) padding: 8px 8px 8px 22px (extra room on the left for the wordmark) radius: 9999px Logo: swap the bold "Swayat AI" for the lowercase wordmark swayat. in font-weight: 500, letter-spacing: -0.02em, with the existing 'S' glyph in a 24×24 ink-black rounded-square (radius: 7px). Nav split: primary links (Features ▾ · Pricing · Blog) on the left next to the logo; secondary (Book a demo · Sign in) as ghost pills on the right with hover: rgba(15,23,42,0.05); the "Get started" CTA stays last but switch its background from --primary (indigo) to --ink (#0a0a0a) so it carries weight against the cream page. Why: the cream hero needs the header to feel like chrome floating on it, not a slab cutting it in half.

"Frame the hero video like a piece of work, not a banner."

Context: the section currently is <section class="flex min-h-screen flex-col items-center"> with a naked <video> and a "Get Started" button below. Target: rebuild as a dark staged showcase: section bg: #0a0a0a padding: 96px clamp(24px, 5vw, 64px) + radial-gradient halos: indigo top-left (rgba(99,102,241,0.30), blur 140px), cyan bottom-right (rgba(6,182,212,0.25), blur 140px) + faint grid: 60×60 lines at rgba(255,255,255,0.04), masked with a radial fade Composition: a max-width: 1180px column inside the dark stage, structured top-to-bottom: 1. Top row — small eyebrow ("live demo · 12 seconds" in indigo-300, uppercase, .24em tracking) on the left; two stats on the right (4,200+ sites built · 0 dashboards) in white over .55-opacity captions. 2. Headline — Instrument Serif, 44–56px, "One message in. A live website out." with the second line italic and 70% opacity. 3. The video, framed — wrap it in a browser-chrome card: - outer: rounded-2xl, 1px rgba(255,255,255,0.08), glow shadow 0 60px 120px -30px rgba(99,102,241,0.45), 0 0 0 1px rgba(99,102,241,0.18) inset - chrome bar: 3 muted dots + a pill URL (emma-babyshower.swayat.com) with a green live dot - the video keeps its 16:10 aspect, fills the card body 4. Caption row — a 380px lede on the left ("Watch Swayat parse a WhatsApp message..."); white pill CTA on the right ("Get started free →"). Remove the existing trio of blur halos — the new stage has its own. Keep the video's autoplay loop muted playsinline attributes. Why: a naked video on a white page reads like a marketing GIF. Framed in a dark stage with chrome, headline, and stats, it reads like the product itself.
